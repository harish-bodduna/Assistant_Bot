### External SharePoint Access (for users outside of WSNS)

#### 1) Open the SharePoint invitation email and click the site link (e.g., **“Go To 1440 Marketing External”**).
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_1_page_1.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A03Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A03Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=QD0bPOrbjRcsEvDs0lBOJcdAjWsnOx90wJWlSPIn/ZA%3D)

#### 2) On the Microsoft sign-in screen, request a one-time code to your email by clicking **Send code**.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_2_page_1.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=QEx8LA7Qg3/z%2BTV0hNQ99JJF%2BX2HDG9pZCkNerUKeEk%3D)

#### 3) Enter the code you received by email, then click **Sign in**.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_3_page_2.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=VtfhHzoXY009f98z5BswHkmrwfm/eVNK09G6I23w%2BZU%3D)

#### 4) When prompted **“More information required”**, click **Next** to begin Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) setup.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_4_page_2.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=lIg/UVXHbHm1GpP3o8h6NfHYl3y7qYOn2a94s%2BFIoPo%3D)

#### 5) When **Permissions requested by 1440 Foods** appears, click **Accept**.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_5_page_2.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=/piCAcZWBuWn3z4YbciEgBnWIr6tSTeTcc6EWM%2B41%2BU%3D)

#### 6) On **Keep your account secure**, either:
- proceed with **Microsoft Authenticator** by clicking **Next**, or  
- choose another option by clicking **I want to set up a different method** (e.g., text message).
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_6_page_2.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=SdxfTzmclQTFrf3zrIzpwipJueTYk%2BRcA1T5Y9Jl0hg%3D)

#### 7) If using Microsoft Authenticator, follow the prompt to set up your account, then click **Next**.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_7_page_2.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=C2CCWorqm8jjFAgYqgVFMyPyZFA6U3EeiHxZEGCH2gw%3D)

#### 8) Scan the displayed QR code using the Microsoft Authenticator app on your phone.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_8_page_3.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=ZG87SinLm%2B13KRpVS0xkjIl0Q01JYUQaawuKhFG9ozI%3D)

#### 9) After successful sign-in and MFA setup, you will be directed to the SharePoint page/site.
![Visual](https://copilot14.blob.core.windows.net/manual-images/External_SharePoint_access/images/fig_9_page_3.png?st=2026-01-09T16%3A43%3A04Z&se=2026-02-08T16%3A48%3A04Z&sp=r&sv=2026-02-06&sr=b&sig=DtGPN1fFtyEF8LWi26txtnNGGCGUZ9AbAjZQiqnYcdE%3D)
